Chase Bank

A professional looking website from a bank. You can tell that a team of people work together to keep this site up to date and stylish.

  • Contrast
  • Repetition
  • Alignment
  • Proximity

Chase Bank's webpage shows contrast with there use of colors which makes it simple to navigate. They show repetition by bringing up checking and credit cards quite often in the page. they also use the same color layout through the webpage. The page is aligned well the space between the pictues are also perfectly spaced. the proximity of the page is set up nice and clean with the elements of the page are spaced well and not to near or far from each other.
